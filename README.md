
# VibeCheck - Aesthetic Social Media Companion

An innovative social media platform designed around aesthetic curation and cultural discovery. VibeCheck enables users to curate, share, and connect with others who share similar aesthetic preferences across cinema, music, gaming, literature, and travel.

## Overview

VibeCheck transforms how people express their tastes and discover kindred spirits. Rather than following traditional social hierarchies, users build an "Aura" ‚Äî a unique aesthetic fingerprint based on their curated interests. The platform combines personalized content curation, social discovery, gamification, and community spaces to create a vibrant, taste-driven social experience.
